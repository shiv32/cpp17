Observer Design Pattern

Intent: Lets you define a subscription mechanism to notify multiple objects about any events that happen to the object they're observing.

Note that there's a lot of different terms with similar meaning associated with this pattern. Just remember that the Subject is also called the Publisher and the Observer is often called the Subscriber and vice versa. Also the verbs "observe", "listen" or "track" usually mean the same thing.
